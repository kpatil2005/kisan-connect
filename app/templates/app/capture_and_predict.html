🌱 Plant Disease Prediction System 🌿

🍅 Tomato Late Blight

#Tomato #Fungal

🟢 Symptoms: Dark brown spots on leaves & stems.

⚠️ Cause: Phytophthora infestans fungus.

💊 Control: Use copper fungicides, remove infected plants.

🥒 Powdery Mildew

#Cucumber #Fungal

🟢 Symptoms: White powdery patches on leaves.

⚠️ Cause: Erysiphe fungus.

💊 Control: Neem oil spray, sulfur-based fungicides.

🌾 Rice Blast

#Rice #Fungal

🟢 Symptoms: Diamond-shaped lesions on leaves.

⚠️ Cause: Magnaporthe oryzae fungus.

💊 Control: Avoid excess nitrogen, apply tricyclazole fungicide.
